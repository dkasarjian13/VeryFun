After numerous releases with Chainsaw Corp, 100% SILK and singles on Alpha Pup with producer Cavalier. Bludwork has reached within for his first ever release as a vocalist called "Painkiller", with all lyrics written and performed by him, its a treat to hear the composer showcasing his singing talent over original productions.

The first 3 songs were Co-Produced by long time collaborator Xendé La Silva, the two songsmiths bonded over Michael Sembello and Sade songs, which they combined with their unique styles to create the songs Devoted, Awakening and Purpose. On this project Bludwork takes his swing at the genres R&B and Soul, taking them to corners within electronic music they've never been before. These 5 tracks were selected after a long period of vocal discovery and exploration. Mostly focusing on themes of loneliness and isolation. Bludwork cites his musical inspirations as all over the board. Listening from Vanessa Bell Armstrong to Gil Scott-Heron. "I wanted to call it Painkiller because I often think about the things people do to ease their pain (mental or physical). I think more than substances can be Painkillers, music is my painkiller, exercise is some peoples while love and affection works for others." - Bludwork

For the cover art, Bludwork joined with graphic designer Devon Kasarjian. Devon's inspiration for the cover art was looking at parts of the brain that specifically react to loneliness, he then took this information and processed it in a unique and striking way for this Sci-Fi Horror type imagery. "Painkiller" was recorded all at Chainsaw Corp. studio's in Santa Clarita, with vocals Mixed by Clayton Burton and entirely mastered by Clayton Burton as well. With his extensive knowledge of live sound and mastering. Clayton and Bludwork were able to come together to fit the vocals within the track, but also allow them to shine among the complex production.
