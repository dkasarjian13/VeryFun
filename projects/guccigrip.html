The Face: born in London in 1980, the original, definitive style magazine. Reborn for 2019. Endless discovery for a new generation. Launched by Nick Logan in Lon­don in 1980, The Face is the orig­i­nal, defin­i­tive style magazine. From the begin­ning it was a cul­tur­al trail­blaz­er, cov­er­ing music, fash­ion, film, TV, soci­ety, pol­i­tics and glob­al cur­rent affairs. And it cov­ered them with inven­tion, inno­va­tion, wit and class – and not to men­tion with ground­break­ing graph­ic design. Now, in 2019, The Face is reborn. A new team with a new vision for a new age, but proud­ly retain­ing the magazine’s found­ing vision and core prin­ci­pals. A for­ward-think­ing, mul­ti-plat­form title stay­ing true to Logan’s pio­neer­ing spirit. The Face is back. Still orig­i­nal, still defin­i­tive. End­less dis­covery for a new generation.
